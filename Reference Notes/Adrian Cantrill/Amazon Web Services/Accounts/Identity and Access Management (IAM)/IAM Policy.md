Also called a policy or document

[[Identity and Access Management Basics|IAM]] allows you to create a policy document which can be used to allow or deny access to AWS services when and only when they're attached to [[Identity and Access Management Basics|IAM]] [[Reference Notes/Adrian Cantrill/Amazon Web Services/Accounts/Identity and Access Management (IAM)/Types of IAM users/User]]s, [[Group]]s, or [[Role]]s.

On their own and without being attached to any entity, they do nothing.
