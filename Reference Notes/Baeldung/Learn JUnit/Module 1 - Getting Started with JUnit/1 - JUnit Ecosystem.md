- JUnit is a lightweight unit-testing framework for [[Notes/Java/Java|Java]]
- It is the most popular at the time of writing
- Latest version is JUnit 6 as of 2026-02-18

Due to the popularity, a lot of plugins and libraries can be used with it. This includes Mockito, a mocking framework

- One of the alternatives of JUnit is TestNG, which is inspired by JUnit. Although it is newer, recent versions of JUnit have caught up to it and thus, JUnit remains the most popular of its kind.
