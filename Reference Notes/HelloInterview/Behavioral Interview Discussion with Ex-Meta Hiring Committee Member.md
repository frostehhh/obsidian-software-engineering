# Importance of Behavioral Interviews
- There will be a clearer distinction of a candidate's level in these interviews since they will be talking about their personal experiences.
- On the contrary, system design interviews for candidates of different levels may look similar, and not convincing enough to indicate the candidate's level

# Evaluating Candidates in Behavioral Interviews
- They look at specific aspects
	- initiative
	- perseverance
	- conflict resolution
	- leadership
	- communication skills
	- handling ambiguity

# Understanding Interviewing Signals
- Good to journal your work - note talking points for interview
- High impact -> good stories

# Presenting Your Experience Effectively
- Simple context is okay. Your interviewer is most likely very highly experienced
- Action is most important
