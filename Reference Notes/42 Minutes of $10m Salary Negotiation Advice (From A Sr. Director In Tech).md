# Why Negotiate
- Generally, more likely to get a higher compensation by negotiating BEFORE you get a job compared to after you get it.
- Companies are more likely to base your raises on your current salary systematically
- You can exponentially increase your salary this way


# Winning Factors for Negotiation
- Avoid desperation
- Research the possible salary levels before hand

# No other offers? Forms of leverage
- In case you have offers, use those as leverage for your target company. Focus on getting leverage from companies that provide similar compensation structure
- If you do have no offers
	- No offers, but I can wait for a better one
	- No offers, but I want this
