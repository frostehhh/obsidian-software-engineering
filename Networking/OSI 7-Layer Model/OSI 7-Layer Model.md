The OSI 7-Layer [[Networking]] model consists of the following layers:
1. [[Physical]] - hardware
1. [[Data Link]]
1. [[Network]]
1. [[Transport]]
1. [[Session]]
1. [[Presentation]]
1. [[Application]] - e.g. web browsers
