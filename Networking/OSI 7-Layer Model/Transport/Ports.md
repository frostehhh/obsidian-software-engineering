A port can be treated as a gateway to the internet. It facilitates in identifying application or service running in browser/computer in same fashion as [IP address](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/introduction-of-classful-ip-addressing/) does.

**Port numbers** are also of different types and have unique numbers. The ports co-exist within an IP address because each and every service has its unique port.

> [!info] 
> Top firms like Google or MSN use well-known ports (0 to 10231) for their services; Certain specific functions are assigned registered ports(1024 to 49151) when requested; There are also private or dynamic ports(49152 to 65535) which can be used by anyone who wishes for private servers and temporary purposes.
